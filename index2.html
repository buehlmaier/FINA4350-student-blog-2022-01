HKU FINA4350 Student Blog

Blogging About Text Analytics and NLP in Finance and Fintech

This blog is written by students at the University of Hong Kong (HKU). It serves two purposes: First, to document students' reflections about their learning progress. Second, to showcase some of the results students have obtained as part of their projects. The blog post contents do not necessarily reflect the opinion(s) of the course instructor or HKU.

The topics of the blog posts are about text analytics and natural language processing (NLP) in finance and fintech. At any rate, keep in mind that this blog is "work in progress." The blog is updated throughout the course as new blog posts become available.

The list of blog posts below only shows the most recent posts. To view all blog posts, click on "all posts" below.
