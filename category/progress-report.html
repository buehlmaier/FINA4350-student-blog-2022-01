HKU FINA4350 Student Blog

Blogging About Text Analytics and NLP in Finance and Fintech

This blog is written by students at the University of Hong Kong (HKU). It serves two purposes: First, to document students' reflections about their learning progress. Second, to showcase some of the results students have obtained as part of their projects.

The topics of the blog posts are about text analytics and natural language processing (NLP) in finance and fintech. At any rate, keep in mind that this blog is "work in progress." The blog will be updated throughout the course as new blog posts become available.

Names of student groups (in order of sign-up):

  • Nebula
  • Python at Large

  • NLPQuartet
  • Metatext
  • Hanna (name TBD)
